Our Story

Sunrise Cafe started in 2010 with a simple mission: create a warm, welcoming space where the community could gather and enjoy exceptional coffee and food made with care and quality ingredients.

What began as a small corner coffee shop has grown into a neighborhood institution, but our values remain the same. Every cup of coffee is brewed with precision, every meal prepared with attention to detail.
